About VS Benefits

Advice that belongs to you, not the carrier.

VS Benefits is an independent health insurance advisory based in Miami, FL. We help small employers (10-99 employees), self-employed operators, and families design benefits that hold up in real life.

Why this practice exists.

Most small business owners get health insurance quotes from someone who will only show them the easy options. Renewal comes, rates go up, nothing gets shopped. It has been that way for decades.

VS Benefits is built around a different idea: every client should see the full market, understand the trade-offs, and keep the same advisor year after year. That is the job. Not the add-ons.

We specialize in the 10-99 employee range because that is where thoughtful design actually moves the needle. Below 10, the options narrow. Above 100, the big firms pay attention. In between, you need an advisor who will do the homework.
