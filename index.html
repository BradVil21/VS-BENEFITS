Common questions

Frequently asked

Do I pay you anything for your help?

No. As an independent advisor, I'm compensated by the carriers when you enroll. My consultation, plan comparisons, paperwork, and ongoing support are free to you.

How is this different from going direct to the carrier?

Going direct only shows you that one carrier's plans. I show you the full market across major carriers and pick what actually fits your family or business — not what one company has to sell.

What states do you serve?

We are licensed in 40+ states. We're based in Miami, FL but work with clients nationwide. Reach out and we will confirm if I'm licensed in yours.

When can I enroll?

Open Enrollment runs Nov 1 - Jan 15 each year. Outside that window you typically need a Special Enrollment qualifying event. Group plans for businesses can be set up year-round.

What if I already have coverage?

We will review what you have at no cost and only recommend a change if it would actually improve your coverage or cost. Often the answer is "you're in the right place, here's why."
