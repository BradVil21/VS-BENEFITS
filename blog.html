ACA Open Enrollment 2027: your plain-English checklist

Open Enrollment for 2027 ACA coverage runs November 1 through January 15, 2027. Miss that window and you will need a qualifying life event to make any changes until 2028. This checklist walks you through the key dates, how to estimate your subsidy using your Modified Adjusted Gross Income, what to watch for with narrow networks that look affordable but leave your doctor out of pocket, and the five mistakes we see clients make every single November. If you are self-employed, between jobs, or buying coverage for your family outside of an employer plan, bookmark this before November 1st. The subsidy cliffs alone could cost you hundreds per month if you guess wrong on your income estimate.

Miami small business health insurance: what is different here

Buying group health insurance in Miami is not the same as buying it in Atlanta or Chicago. If you are a small business owner in South Florida weighing your options, this is written specifically for your situation.
