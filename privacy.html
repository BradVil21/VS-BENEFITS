Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

VS Benefits ("VS Benefits," "we," "our," or "us") is an independent health insurance advisory based in Miami, Florida. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use vshealthbenefits.com (the "Site"), request a quote, contact us, or use our Client or Admin portals, and how we handle that information.

The short version: We collect what you give us when you ask for a quote or get in touch. We use it to advise you and place coverage with carriers you choose. We do not sell your information. We share it only with the carriers and tools that are necessary to provide you with insurance options or run our practice.

1. Information we collect

We collect information in three ways: information you give us, information collected automatically, and information we receive from third parties.

Information you give us

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number, ZIP code, mailing address, and similar information you submit through forms on the Site.
  • Coverage information: the type of coverage you are exploring (individual, family, employer/group, Medicare, ACA, dental/vision, etc.), household size, employee count, current carrier, effective dates, and similar facts you share so we can provide accurate options.
  • Health-related details you choose to share: general health questions, prescriptions, providers, or chronic conditions you mention so we can match you to plan networks and benefits. You are not required to share more than you are comfortable with.
  • Account information: if you create a Client or Admin portal account, we collect a username, email, password (stored hashed), and any documents or notes you upload.
  • Communications: emails, text messages, and call notes when you correspond with us.

Information collected automatically

  • IP address, device type, browser, operating system, referring page, pages viewed, and time stamps.
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies & analytics).

Information from third parties

  • Health insurance carriers, general agencies, and quoting platforms when we run quotes on your behalf.
  • Service providers we use to operate the Site and our practice (for example, hosting, form delivery, scheduling, e-signature, and analytics tools).

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and prepare side-by-side quotes;
  • Submit applications to carriers you authorize;
  • Service your policy year-round (claims questions, renewals, ID cards, ICHRA setup, etc.);
  • Operate the Site and our portals, including troubleshooting, fraud prevention, and security;
  • Send transactional messages (appointment confirmations, document requests, renewal reminders);
  • Send occasional educational or marketing messages, which you can opt out of at any time;
  • Comply with our legal, regulatory, and licensing obligations as a licensed insurance producer.

3. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as follows:

  • Insurance carriers and general agencies when you ask us to quote, apply for, or enroll in coverage. Each carrier handles your information under its own privacy practices.
  • Service providers who help us run the practice, including hosting (Vercel), database/auth (Google Firebase), form delivery and workflow automation (Make.com), scheduling (Google Calendar), email (Google Workspace and similar), and analytics. These providers process your information only on our instructions.
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting, compliance) under confidentiality obligations.
  • Government agencies and regulators where required for licensing, marketplace enrollment (e.g., HealthCare.gov), or compliance.
  • If required by law, in response to subpoenas, court orders, or to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

4. HIPAA & protected health information

VS Benefits is a licensed insurance producer. In some interactions we may handle information that resembles protected health information ("PHI"). We treat any health-related information you share with us as confidential and use it only to advise you on coverage and to communicate with carriers you authorize. We do not disclose health-related information to third parties for marketing.

If you are an employer-client and we act as your group's broker of record, additional confidentiality and HIPAA-related obligations may apply under our written agreement and the carrier's terms.

5. Cookies, analytics & tracking

The Site uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you signed into the Client/Admin portals, measure traffic, and improve the experience. We may use:

  • Strictly necessary cookies (login sessions, security).
  • Performance and analytics cookies (anonymous traffic and usage statistics).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit portal functionality.

6. Your rights & choices

You can:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate information;
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to record-retention rules that apply to licensed insurance producers;
  • Opt out of marketing emails or text messages by replying STOP, clicking the unsubscribe link, or emailing us;
  • Withdraw consent for us to continue acting on your behalf at any time.

Residents of California, Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, Utah, Texas, Oregon, and other states with comprehensive privacy laws may have additional rights. Email us at bvilsainthealth@gmail.com to exercise any of these rights, and we will respond within the period required by applicable law.

7. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide our services and as required by state and federal record-keeping rules that apply to licensed insurance producers (typically several years after the policy ends or the relationship concludes). When we no longer need your information, we delete or de-identify it.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information in our care, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS), access controls on our portals, and reputable hosting and database providers. No system is perfectly secure; if you suspect that your account or information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. Children's privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, communicated to you directly.
